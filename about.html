Our Story

Twelve years of showing up
where it counts.

We built Golden Marketing to solve a problem we saw everywhere: brands investing heavily in media but losing ground at the shelf, on the street, and in the market. Field marketing done right changes that.

Who we are

Golden Marketing Uganda is a full-service field marketing and brand activation agency headquartered in Kampala, with active operations across Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, and the Democratic Republic of Congo.

We exist to bridge the gap between brand strategy and real-world execution. While agencies craft compelling campaigns, we ensure those campaigns land — at the right shelf, with the right consumer, in the right moment. Our people are trained brand ambassadors, logistics specialists, and field analysts who work together to deliver measurable impact.

Over 12 years, we have partnered with multinationals, regional players, and high-growth startups alike. The common thread: brands that need more than creative — they need results at ground level.

Our journey

Built milestone by milestone

2012

Founded in Kampala

Started with a single client and a five-person team focused on FMCG merchandising across Kampala's central markets.

2015

East Africa expansion

Extended operations into Kenya and Tanzania, tripling headcount and adding large-format roadshow capabilities.

2019

Technology integration

Launched our proprietary field automation and real-time reporting platform, giving clients live visibility into every activation.

2024

Central Africa presence

Added Rwanda and DRC to our active markets, completing a six-country footprint across East and Central Africa.
