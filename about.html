Our Story

15 years of showing up
where it counts.

Golden Marketing is here to address a critical gap in the marketing landscape: brands investing significantly in campaigns, yet failing to convert that investment into meaningful visibility, engagement, and sales where it matters most, at the shelf, on the street, and in the marketplace. We believe that when executed strategically, field marketing bridges this gap, transforming brand awareness into measurable business results.

Who we are

Field marketing that moves the market

Golden Marketing Uganda is a Below The Line marketing and brand activation agency headquartered in Kampala, delivering integrated marketing solutions across Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, and the Democratic Republic of Congo.

We partner with ambitious brands to drive market penetration, enhance consumer engagement, and deliver measurable commercial impact through strategic, data-driven field execution. Our people are trained brand ambassadors, logistics specialists, and field analysts who turn campaign strategy into results at ground level.

Our journey

Built milestone by milestone

From a five-person team in Kampala to a six-country field force — how the last decade and a half took shape.

2012

Founded in Kampala

Started with a single client and a five-person team focused on FMCG merchandising across Kampala's central markets.

2015

East Africa expansion

Extended operations into Kenya and Tanzania, tripling headcount and adding large-format roadshow capabilities.

2019

Technology integration

Launched our proprietary field automation and real-time reporting platform, giving clients live visibility into every activation.

2022

Field force at scale

Grew to 2,000+ trained brand ambassadors and field agents, powering nationwide modern- and general-trade coverage for our clients.

2024

Central Africa footprint

Added Rwanda and the DRC to our active markets, completing a six-country footprint across East and Central Africa.

2026

Fifteen years strong

Today, 2,000+ field agents activate 50+ brands across six markets — every deployment backed by real-time reporting and measurable commercial results.
