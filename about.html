13 Years of Execution.

From a local Kampala agency to a pan-East African field marketing powerhouse.

The Golden Standard.

Founded in 2013, Golden Marketing Uganda was built on a simple premise: brands were spending millions on strategy but losing at the point of sale due to poor field execution.

Today, we bridge that gap. We don't just plan; we deploy thousands of trained personnel across 6 countries, armed with proprietary digital tools to ensure your product is visible, compliant, and moving off the shelves.

Our Journey

2013

Inception

Golden Marketing established in Kampala, Uganda, primarily focusing on local BTL activations and roadshows.

2016

The FMCG Shift

Secured major contracts with global FMCG brands, expanding our services heavily into merchandising and retail audits.

2019

Digital Transformation

Launched our proprietary Field Force Automation app, moving 100% of our reporting to real-time digital dashboards.

2023+

Regional Expansion

Fully operational capabilities established across Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Zambia, and Mozambique.
