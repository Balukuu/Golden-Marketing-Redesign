Service

Retail Audit

Unbiased, systematic in-store data that tells you exactly what is happening at the shelf — distribution rates, pricing compliance, POSM presence, and share of shelf across your trade universe.

What it includes

  • Distribution checks — numeric and weighted distribution by outlet type
  • Shelf price tracking and competitor price benchmarking
  • Share of shelf measurement against key competitors
  • POSM compliance — presence, placement, and condition of branded materials
  • Out-of-stock identification with root cause classification
  • Photo evidence for every audit data point
  • Monthly or quarterly trend reports for KPI tracking over time

How it works

Our process

01

Outlet universe & questionnaire design

We work with you to define the outlet sample — trade type, geography, and size — and design a questionnaire that captures exactly the KPIs your commercial team tracks.

02

Field data collection

Trained audit agents visit each outlet in the sample, collecting data on mobile devices with time and geo-stamps. Every data point is backed by a photograph, eliminating ambiguity.

03

Analysis & trend reporting

We clean, analyse, and present the data in a structured report. Recurring audits are benchmarked against previous waves so you can see the trajectory — not just a snapshot.
