Service

Field Activations

We take your brand off the shelf and put it directly in the hands of consumers. Sampling events, demonstrations, and experiential campaigns that drive trial and loyalty.

What it includes

  • Product sampling and trial generation at high-traffic locations
  • Trained brand ambassadors who know your product inside and out
  • Live product demonstrations and consumer education sessions
  • School activations, market activations, and community events
  • Consumer feedback collection via digital forms during each activation
  • Full logistics support — setup, teardown, and equipment storage
  • Post-activation reports with consumer reach, samples distributed, and key insights

How it works

Our process

01

Brief & location strategy

We review your campaign objectives, target consumer profile, and geography. Then we identify the highest-value activation sites — where your audience actually is.

02

Team training & execution

Ambassadors go through a brand-specific training before any consumer contact. Every activation day is supervised, photo-documented, and tracked against a daily KPI sheet.

03

Consumer insight & reporting

Every activation generates structured consumer feedback. We compile reach data, qualitative insights, and photographic evidence into a campaign wrap-up report you can present to stakeholders.
