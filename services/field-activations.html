My Shell Club: Loyalty Programme Launch

12,000+
6
4
3x
200+

The Challenge: Shell Uganda needed to rapidly drive awareness and enrolments for their new My Shell Club loyalty app across Uganda's major cities — with a target of 4,000 sign-ups in one month.


Our Approach: We deployed over 200 trained brand ambassadors to major Shell forecourts in Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja, Mbarara, Gulu and Mbale. Each ambassador was equipped with branded tablets, uniform, and a structured engagement script. They intercepted drivers during the natural pause of refuelling — the perfect moment for a product conversation.


The Result: In just 4 weeks, we surpassed the original target by 3 times — driving over 12,000 verified app enrolments and establishing My Shell Club as one of Uganda's fastest-growing fuel loyalty programmes.

The Challenge: Coca-Cola Uganda wanted to re-energise consumer connection with the brand across key urban and peri-urban markets in Central, Eastern, Western and Northern Uganda — particularly targeting 18—35 year olds.


Our Approach: We ran simultaneous activations across universities, busy taxi parks, shopping centres, and community markets. Branded experiential zones were set up at each location with chilled product, branded photo booths, and interactive consumer mechanics. Our teams captured consumer data and drove social sharing through branded hashtag moments.


The Result: Over 80,000 units were sampled across 150+ unique touchpoints in 12 weeks. Post-campaign brand recall surveys showed 95% unaided brand awareness among those who experienced the activation, with a measurable uplift in retail offtake in activated markets.

Simu Ya Jamii: Rural Connectivity Road Show

22
35,000+
8
12
180+

The Challenge: Airtel Uganda needed to penetrate underserved rural and peri-urban districts with their affordable smartphone and data bundle offering, driving SIM registration and network adoption outside major cities.


Our Approach: We assembled a convoy of 12 fully branded vehicles — each equipped with a generator, sound system, branded gazebos, and a team of 8 field agents. The convoy moved through 22 districts over 8 weeks, staging community events at trading centres, weekly markets, and district headquarters. On-ground activities included live demos, device financing sign-ups, and data bundle activations managed via digital POS systems.


The Result: Over 35,000 SIM activations were recorded, with an average of 1,600 per district visit. The campaign generated significant earned media in local radio and community social groups, amplifying reach well beyond direct touchpoints.
