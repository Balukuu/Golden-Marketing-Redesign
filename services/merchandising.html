Service

Merchandising

We put your products in the right place, at the right time, in every outlet that matters. Consistent shelf presence that drives purchase decisions.

What it includes

  • Shelf arrangement and planogram compliance across all assigned outlets
  • POSM placement and maintenance — shelf wobblers, talkers, and floor standees
  • Out-of-stock identification and escalation to sales teams
  • Competitor activity monitoring and shelf share reporting
  • Photo-verified compliance reporting delivered in real time
  • Coverage across supermarkets, dukas, pharmacies, petrol forecourts, and kiosks
  • Trained field teams with brand-specific onboarding for every campaign

How it works

Our process

01

Outlet mapping & planning

We audit your target outlets, agree on planogram standards, and assign dedicated merchandisers to each territory. Coverage plans are agreed before any boots hit the ground.

02

Daily execution & compliance

Merchandisers visit every assigned outlet on the agreed schedule. Each visit is logged with geo-tagged photos and compliance scores captured in our field app in real time.

03

Reporting & continuous improvement

You receive weekly dashboards showing compliance rates, share of shelf, out-of-stock incidents, and competitor activity. We review together and adjust coverage continuously.
