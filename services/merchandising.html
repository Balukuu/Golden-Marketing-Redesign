The Challenge: MTN Uganda needed to rapidly deploy a new seasonal campaign's POSM suite — banners, shelf wobblers, counter mats, and branded stands — across over 2,800 airtime retail points in 6 districts, with zero tolerance for outdated materials in-store.


Our Approach: We mobilised 200+ field agents across all districts simultaneously, using a wave deployment model that prioritised high-traffic urban outlets first before moving to secondary and rural points. Each installation was photo-verified and logged to a shared tracking dashboard accessible to MTN's trade marketing team in real time.


The Result: Full rollout completed in 72 hours across 2,800+ outlets. MTN's trade marketing team reported 98% material uptime over the 3-month campaign window — the highest compliance rate in the programme's history.

The Challenge: Stanbic Bank Uganda needed a reliable partner to manage in-branch presentation standards across all 85 branches — ensuring product brochures, digital screen content, queue management materials, and branch branding were always current, correctly placed, and on-brand.


Our Approach: We assigned dedicated merchandisers to branch clusters, each conducting weekly visits with a standardised 45-point compliance checklist.
