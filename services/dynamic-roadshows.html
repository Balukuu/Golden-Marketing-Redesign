Service

Dynamic Roadshows

Your campaign moves with your consumer. We deploy fully branded activation vehicles and expert teams across multiple markets in a single campaign wave.

What it includes

  • Fully branded roadshow vehicles with on-board activation equipment
  • Multi-city route planning across Uganda and cross-border markets
  • Live music, entertainment, and crowd engagement to build event energy
  • Product sampling, demos, and on-the-spot consumer engagement
  • PA system, tents, branded gazebos, and branded uniforms supplied
  • Real-time GPS tracking and daily reporting on reach and coverage
  • End-of-tour campaign report with market-level consumer data

How it works

Our process

01

Route design & permitting

We map the roadshow route across target towns and markets, secure all necessary venue and event permits, and arrange accommodation and logistics for the touring team.

02

Activation setup & execution

Our team arrives at each location ahead of schedule, sets up the branded environment, and runs the full day's activation programme. Every stop is documented with photos and reach tallies.

03

Market-level reporting

A post-roadshow report covers each town visited, consumer numbers reached, samples distributed, and key consumer feedback themes — giving you a market-by-market performance picture.
